about=GoogleMapsDock is a QGIS plugin that opens Google Maps in a dockable browser panel when you click on the QGIS map canvas. 
 
 It centers the view on the clicked location and allows full Street View exploration directly within QGIS.  

 Coordinates are automatically transformed to WGS84 (EPSG:4326) for compatibility with Google Maps.  

 Ideal for digitizing road networks or verifying features using real-world imagery.
